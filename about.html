About

The Tommy Kay Experience is the solo project of the German Rock musician Dominik Kimmel (Turn The Course; Senseless Revolution). Launched at the end of 2020, the project stands for especially one thing: Having fun with music! The Tommy Kay Experience delivers a decent mix of Nu Metal, Alternative Metal and Alternative Rock with cracking guitar riffs, catchy melodies, and vocal variety (from rap to clean vocals to shouts). There was already a first sign of life with the single "Chances" in March 2021. With his album called "Sitdrub", which will be released in autumn, Dominik Kimmel now wants to follow up and set a milestone in his career so far. The first album for both himself and The Tommy Kay Experience! This is also intended to lay the foundation stone that will pave the way for the new path, because we can still expect a lot in the future! Fans of music from the early 2000s should definitely take a closer look at his music.
